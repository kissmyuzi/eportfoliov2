SUMMER STUDIO: Expo- Day

Final Thoughts:

Over the last month, I have been involved with an intensive subject: “Cyber Security: An Offensive Mindset” during summer. Summer studio is a project-based subject aimed for students from different faculties at the University of Technology Sydney, where they can engage in real-world challenges. Utilising a flexible teaching framework, the studios are facilitated by a mix of academic experts and industry partners.

It has been a great pleasure being a part of a cohort that despite being a year or two my senior, established a highly energetic and collaborative environment for the duration of the studio. Due to the flexible teaching format, we were urged to explore our own interests within the Cyber Security field.

Thanks to the amazing opportunities provided by the education team, the past four weeks were quite eventful. From being taught hands-on penetration testing by Deloitte’s security team, to undertaking a Reverse-Engineering Workshop ran by Ruben from Symantec. I was able to gain invaluable insights of the industry and in my spare time, test real-world web applications within legal boundaries.

Overall, I am extremely proud of what I have achieved in this studio, and last Friday marked the final studio day where we presented our journey to other students and teachers. Special thanks to the head educators: Larry and Darsh for their genuine interest to keep up with every student, and the helpful tutors: Luke and Jai, for putting up with my countless questions. #cybersecurity #summerstudio #pentest
