Professional Assessment Activity 2

Peer Review

On the final day of EPP1 we were tasked again to review the E-portfolios of our peers. Similar to Professional Assessment Activity 1, we followed the strict “PIP” format to give our feedback. The first requirement for this week was the the completion of the “Ethics” and “Workplace health and safety” section, followed by more critical incident journals since assessment 1.

For this assessment, the peers I reviewed are: Nathan, Bilal and Lancelot.

It was great to see the improvements my peers achieved since last assessment but a trend of missing content was noticable for all the peers I have reviewed. Thus, I have urged all of them to complete them before the assessment date. Other than that, all the portfolios were fantastic in showcasing the professional readiness of my peers.

For my feedback, I was notified to change the format for some of my journals as there was the case of too many topics in one journal. So my current plan is to restructure them into seperate journals with seperate headlines. The new headlines will include: “EPP1 Final Thoughts”, “Safety and Ethics” and finally “Workplace Learning Readiness”.
